St. James's March 19th. 1767

Sir,

I am directed by General Conway to send You the inclos'd Memorial of M. de Walderen, the Dutch Minister, with regard to the Affair of M. Van Teytingen, together with the inclos'd Documents concerning it. These You are desired to lay before the Honorable Directors of the East India Company.

 I am &c.

David Hume.
