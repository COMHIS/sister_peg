London 19 Sept 1767

No: Sir Gilbert: With your leave, I did not say, that the Ministry had attaind some sort of Appearance of a kind of Stability, because one Minister was indifferent about Power and another desirous of Retreat, but notwithstanding that Circumstance.

 I have many very important and very interesting Events to communicate to you; but shall omitt it, as you have treated my last Intelligence so ludicrously.
