[le 21 juin 1766]

Mr Hume's Compliments to M. Rousseau. He sends him a letter from Mde de Boufflers; and begs, as soon as convenient, an Answer to his last; as he shall be obliged to leave London soon; and shall not then have it in his Power to be  any longer of Service to him.

Lisle Street Leicester Fields 
 21 of June 1766
