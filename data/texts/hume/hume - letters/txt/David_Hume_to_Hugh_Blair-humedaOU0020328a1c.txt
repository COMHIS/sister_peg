Doncaster 27 of June [1776]

Mr John Hume, alias Home, alias the Home, alias the late Lord Conservator, alias the late Minister of the Gospel at Athelstoneford, has calculated Matters so as to arrive infallibly with his Friend in St David's Street on Wednesday Evening. He has ask'd several of Dr Blair's Friends to dine with him there on Thursday, being the 4th of July; and begs the favour of the Doctor to make one of the Number.
