Sir

I came on Friday & had the Satisfaction of Finding Mr Rouseau in perfect health, he seems to like the place, amuses himself with walking, when the weather is fair, if rainy he plays upon the harpsichord, or writes — is very sociable & an excellent companion; pray remember your promise of Calling, & of giving me as much notice as you Can, in about 10 days I shall go to Davenport, Mr R— stays here, somehow be it at which house it chances, I beg leave to Assure you none will be more pleas'd to See Mr Hume than his Most Obedient

Hble Servt 
Richd Davenport



Wootton May 14th 1766.
