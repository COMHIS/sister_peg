Title : David Hume to Jean Jacques Rousseau
Date : 25 February 1766
Written from:  no location of writing
Written to : Chiswick, Greater London, England, Europe
Editorial project : EE founding corpus
Published online : 2008
Crossref DOI : http://dx.doi.org/10.13051/ee:doc/humedaOU0020017b1c

Mr Hume presents his best Respects to Mr Rousseau. He will have the Honour of waiting on him to morrow forenoon, and of presenting to him Mrs Boscowen,1 Widow of the Admiral, and Sister in law to Mr Price2

Tuesday forenoon [Feb. 1766.]
