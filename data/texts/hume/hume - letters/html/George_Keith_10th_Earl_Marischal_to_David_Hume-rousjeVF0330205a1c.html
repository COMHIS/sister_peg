Title : George Keith, 10th Earl Marischal to David Hume
Date : Tuesday, 7 July 1767
Written from:  Potsdam, Potsdam district, Brandenburg (state), Germany
Written to : no location of receipt
Editorial project : EE founding corpus
Published online : 2008

Potsdam 7 July 1767

. . . I pity Rousseau who thinks there is so generall a conspiracy against him, it is most generous in you to wish to serve him, I would if I knew how, but my advice is of little weight, had it been of any he would have done you justice. He will have his way. Ia not only think General Conway partaker in the plot with you, but even the King joined in it. What a misfortune it is that his life, I mean that of Jean Jaques, and his quiet is poysoned by his distrustfull temper. . . .
