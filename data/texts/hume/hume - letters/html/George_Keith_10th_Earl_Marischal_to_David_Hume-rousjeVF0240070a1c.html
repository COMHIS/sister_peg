Title : George Keith, 10th Earl Marischal to David Hume
Date : Wednesday, 20 February 1765
Written from:  Potsdam, Potsdam district, Brandenburg (state), Germany
Written to : Paris, Ville de Paris (department), Île-de-France, France, Europe
Editorial project : EE founding corpus
Published online : 2008
Crossref DOI : http://dx.doi.org/10.13051/ee:doc/rousjeVF0240070a1c

Potsdam 20 february 1765

Sir

. . . I am sorry poor Jean Jaques can not live quiet in his mountain but is attacked by Voltaire, nothing is more just than your comparison1 of the two combatants; both will receive wounds and hurt without Victory, which will be ascribed (as usuall) to each by their partisans. I have not seen Les Lettres de la Campagne, those de la Montagne are in answer to the first, so J.J. is not the Agressor . . . .
