[début novembre 1766 ]b

[. . .] I am a little uneasy at not hearing from Horace Walpole. It is not possible he can be angry at me for D'Alembert's publishing a Paper1, which I never saw, till I read it in print. I am with great sincerity,

Your affectionate humble Servantc,

David Hume
