Title : David Hume to Andrew Millara
Date : Tuesday, 21 October 1766
Written from:  Edinburgh, Edinburgh (unitary authority), Scotland
Written to : Greater London, England, Europe
Editorial project : EE founding corpus
Published online : 2008
Crossref DOI : http://dx.doi.org/10.13051/ee:doc/rousjeVF0310048a1c

Dear Sir

I fancy Mr Macleane's Questions1 are by this time answerd to his Satisfaction: For my friends at Paris2 have thought it absolutely necessary to publish a Narrative of the whole Affair b between Rousseau and meb, translated from an Account I sent them. I believe the World never heard of so ridiculous and absurd an Affair. [. . .]

Edinburgh 21 of Octr 1766
