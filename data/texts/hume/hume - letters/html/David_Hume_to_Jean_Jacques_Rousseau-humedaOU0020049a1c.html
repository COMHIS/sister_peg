Title : David Hume to Jean Jacques Rousseau
Date : Monday, 26 May 1766
Written from:  Lisle Street, Leicester Fields, City of Westminster, Greater London, England
Written to : no location of receipt
Editorial project : EE founding corpus
Published online : 2008
Crossref DOI : http://dx.doi.org/10.13051/ee:doc/humedaOU0020049a1c

Mr Hume presents his Compliments to M. Rousseau. He has received the enclosed from the English Minister at the Court of Turin.1 He begs to be remembered to Mr Davenport.

Lisle Street Leicester Fields 26 of May 1766
