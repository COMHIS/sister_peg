29 Aprile

. . . I do believe Mr Rousseau will find it impossible to live where he finds no a bodya who understandb a word of what he sais, there occurs so often occasion, even of triffling things necessary, that it is a vexation not to understandsb the language of the country; I feel ita often, tho I understand many words of German . . . .
