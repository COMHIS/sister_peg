Cleveland Row, 30th Aug. 1762

My dear Sir

I am greatly disappointed at Rousseau's not being here2, I should have been extremely happy in showing him every mark of attention in my power upon your account, upon his, & indeed, upon my own, for I admire him, I take your recommendation as a particular Complimt, whether our great folks would think with you or me about him I can't answer for, unless you put Andrew Millar3 in that number . . . .
