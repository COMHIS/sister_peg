New York 9th Novr 1778

Sir,

As Lord Carlisle and Mr Eden are preparing to return to Great Britain, His Majesty's Commissioners have directed me to desire that you will fortwith inform yourself from the Merchants, whether previous to their Excellencies Departure there are any farther measures respecting Trade which are wished to be taken into consideration and I shall be glad to know the result as soon as convenient. I am with ver sincere Respect, Sir, your most obedient and humble servant

Adam Ferguson

Andrew Elliott Esq. 
Superintendent of the Port of New York
