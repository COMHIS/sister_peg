Sir,

I take the Liberty to send you the inclosed Petition concerning a Ship calld the Levant some Flax seed Mahogany Staves & other Goods bought by My Agent Zebadiah Story in August 1777, which are now lying at Rhode Island & cannot get leave to bring them to England. I request the Favour of you to lay the Petition before The Commissioners or any other Person you think proper to obtain an order for me to permit the Vessel to come hither, or if incapable the Goods in any other Vessel that may be bound to Great Britain or Ireland. I had another Ship called the Success which was sunk in August last at Rhode Island.

I have the Honour to be Sir your most Obedt Servant

Edward Mayne

New Broad Street 4th January 1779
