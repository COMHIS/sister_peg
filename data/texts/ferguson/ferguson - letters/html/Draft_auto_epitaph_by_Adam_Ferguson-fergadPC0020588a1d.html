Title : Draft auto-epitaph by Adam Ferguson
Date : before Thursday, 22 February 1816
Written from:  no location of writing
Written to : no location of receipt
Editorial project : EE founding corpus
Published online : 2013
Crossref DOI : http://dx.doi.org/10.13051/ee:doc/fergadPC0020588a1d

As we whose Bones are burried here
Have seen The Works of God
So ye living in your Turn
Behold them an[d] rejoice
The Light is glorious
And if Understood
Will more than Recompence the Pains
you feel. While in this Nursery
of Intellectual Being
your Follies are
with Stripes
Even we whose Bones
are buried in this Ground
Have been Spectators of the Earth
and Heavens. Ye Living in your
Turn behold them & rejoice
The Sight is glorious and if Understood
will more than recompence the Pangs
you feel: while in this Nursery of
Intellectual Beings your Follies
[are repre]sseda with Stripes.
