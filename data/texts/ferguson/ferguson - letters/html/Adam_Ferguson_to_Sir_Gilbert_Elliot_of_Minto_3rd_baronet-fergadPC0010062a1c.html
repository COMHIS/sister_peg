Title : Adam Ferguson to Sir Gilbert Elliot of Minto, 3rd baronet

Written from:  Lauriston, Edinburgh, Edinburgh (unitary authority), Scotland, Europe

[folio 1 recto]

Lauriston1, July 30th
1766

Dear Sir

I set out for the Highlands tomorrow morning with the Mr Grevilles2. I wished to see you that I might receive my Manuscript3 & any remarks you have taken the trouble to make. If you have any that can be put in writing without too much trouble I beg that you will deliver them with the Book itself to Mr Russell the Professor of Natural Philosophy, who will call or send to you for it. I have informed him of my intentions & entrusted him with any negotiations that may arise with Booksellers in my Absence.

I am with the greatest Respect
Dear Sir
your most Obedient &
most humble Servant
Adam Ferguson
