Title : James Cunningham, 14th earl of Glencairn to Adam Ferguson1
Date : Tuesday, 13 May 1783
Written from:  no location of writing
Written to : no location of receipt
Editorial project : EE founding corpus

[folio 1 recto]

Lord Glencairn presents respectful Copts to Professor Ferguson takes the liberty of honouring him with the inclosed2 to be sent to General F. Campbell if he shoud be gone to London it contains letters to be delivered by the General to Lords Graham3 Maitland4 and Mahon.

Edgerston5 May 13

The general will be so good as
Seal the two letters sent open.
LdG.6 cannot send a proper flying
seal as he has not a seal with
his arms here.
