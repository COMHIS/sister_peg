Argyle Sqr Tuesday morning 1st Febry 1785

Dear Sir

I wished to have got Speech of you yesterday to beg the Favour you would help at a party which is to be here at dinner on Friday next. The Company is Mrs Mure1 & one of her Daughters the Principal & his Family, And for your encouragement one of his Sons2 is come from India lately by Land & can tell of the Turks & Saracens so do not fail to engage yourself.

I am but a Sorry Landlord & this weather is likely to make me Still more Sorry

yours most Affectionately
Adam Ferguson
