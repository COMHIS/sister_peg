[folio 1 recto]

19 Feb. 1762a

My Lord

Principal Goldie1 died this morning. I am going to attend My Class Otherwise would have carryed this Message Myself. I will call to Receive your Lordships Commands after twelve O Clock. I am with the greatest respect [folio 1 verso]

your Lordships
most faithful & most
humble Servant
Adam Ferguson

Friday 10 O'Clock
