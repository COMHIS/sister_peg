Title : Adam Ferguson to William Strahan
Date : Thursday, 23 January 1783
Written from:  Edinburgh, Edinburgh (unitary authority), Scotland
Written to : no location of receipt
Editorial project : EE founding corpus
Published online : 2013

[folio 1 recto]

Edinburgh 23d January
1783

Dear Sir

Before I had the very great Satisfaction of your last of the 18th past1, I looked impatiently for the time at which I should repeat my thanks for the continuance of your kind attention to my publication. I am very sensible that if this work should meet with a favourable reception, much will be owing to that attention & to the indulgence with which you favoured me in my manner of treating the proofs. I inclose all that occurs to me for the Title page2. You will please to consider whether the reference to my former publication will help to make [folio 1 verso] me known or any way contribute to set off this. I am


Dear Sir
with the most sincere Respect
& Esteem
your most Obliged
humble Servant
Adam Ferguson
