[folio 1 recto]

London

My Dear Sir

Since I wrote you this morning, by Express2, I received a very discouraging letter from our friend, the C — 13. But I know his character so well, that he will fall in the last Ditch. Time pressed Unexpectedly; and I much regret, that decisive powers, relative to a situation here4, have not been sent. An Express may come too late; yet it may be ventured. Cannot three little fellows5, beat these two overgrown rascals6, that have been so saucy. I am much hurried; and the last bell7, is in the street.

yours &c

August 28th
11 at night
