Title : Adam Ferguson to Andrew Fletcher of Saltoun, Lord Milton

Written from:  Edinburgh, Edinburgh (unitary authority), Scotland

[folio 1 recto]

Edinburgh

My Lord

I have enclosed the letters1 your Lordship gave me and a Short proposal2 which may serve as a Supplement to Dr Wisharts report3. I have been much hurried but hope these papers do not come too late. I am with the greatest Respect

My Lord
your Lordships
most Obliged & most
humble Servant
Adam Ferguson
