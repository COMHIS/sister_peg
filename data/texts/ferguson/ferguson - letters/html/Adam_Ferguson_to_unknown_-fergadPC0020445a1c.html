[folio 1 recto]

Dr Sir

As the Charts belonging to Vancouvers Voyage1 are cumbersome and exposed to damage I have thought it best to have thema & you will accordingly Observe that they are delivered by the bearer. I am Dear Sir

your most obedt
& most humble Servant
Adam Ferguson

Chapel Street2
25 Septr 1798
